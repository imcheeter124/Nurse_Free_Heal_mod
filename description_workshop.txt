[h1]Nurse Free Heal[/h1]

Tired of paying the Nurse every time you need a quick patch-up? This mod makes the Nurse heal you [b]completely free of charge[/b] — just walk up and chat!

[h2]Features[/h2]
[list]
[*] Talk to the Nurse to get healed for free — no gold cost
[*] Removes debuffs on heal, just like the vanilla Nurse
[*] Configurable [b]cooldown[/b] between free heals (default: 60 seconds)
[*] Configurable [b]heal percentage[/b] — restore a portion or all of your missing health (default: 100%)
[*] Optional [b]cooldown reset on boss kill[/b] — defeat a boss and she's ready to go again
[*] On-screen cooldown timer so you always know when she's available
[*] 29 unique snarky Nurse quotes to keep things fresh
[/list]

[h2]Config Options[/h2]
All settings are server-side and configurable in-game via Mod Config:
[list]
[*] [b]Cooldown (seconds)[/b] — 5 to 3600 seconds (default: 60)
[*] [b]Heal Percentage[/b] — 1% to 100% of missing health (default: 100%)
[*] [b]Reset Cooldown on Boss Kill[/b] — off by default
[/list]

[h2]Localization[/h2]
Supports: English, Ukrainian, Russian, German, Spanish
